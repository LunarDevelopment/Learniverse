What is Capsules.io

Capsules,io is a location-based social platform that provides its users the freedom to augment the world around them using virtual containers called capsules. They can be dropped at any location and are accessible to anyone in proximity.

Capsules are containers so you can move them, and pick them up. They are highly customisable through extensions allowing you to configure their visibility and mobility among other things.
